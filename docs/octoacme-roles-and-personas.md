# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Additional Personas (proposed additions)

The following personas extend the core roles to support broader project collaboration.

### Technical Lead (Tech Lead)

#### Role Summary
Oversees technical direction, mentors engineers, and owns architecture. Coordinates technical planning and risk assessments.

#### Responsibilities
- Make architectural decisions and guide technical direction
- Provide technical mentorship and coaching to developers
- Lead design and code reviews
- Escalate technical risks and propose mitigations
- Set and enforce code standards and best practices

#### Interactions
- **Developers**: Coaches and mentors on technical approaches and code quality
- **PM**: Advises on delivery feasibility, timelines, and technical trade-offs
- **PdM**: Consults on technical constraints and solution options during roadmap discussions

---

### UX Designer

#### Role Summary
Owns product usability, accessibility, and interface design. Conducts user research and testing to ensure excellent user experiences.

#### Responsibilities
- Design wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Conduct usability studies and user research
- Deliver design assets and specifications to development
- Advocate for accessibility and inclusive design
- Iterate on designs based on user feedback and testing

#### Interactions
- **PdM**: Collaborates on requirements and user stories to understand user needs
- **Developers**: Partners to ensure designs are implemented accurately and feasibly
- **QA**: Works together on UI testing and visual regression validation

---

### QA Lead / Test Automation Engineer

#### Role Summary
Leads quality assurance efforts and test automation strategy. Ensures comprehensive test coverage and timely defect remediation.

#### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain test automation frameworks
- Author test plans and define testing strategies
- Maintain quality dashboards and metrics
- Support manual QA efforts when needed
- Report on quality status and release readiness

#### Interactions
- **Developers**: Coordinates test requirements and integrates testing into the development workflow
- **PM**: Provides quality status updates and advises on release readiness
- **Tech Lead**: Aligns on code quality standards and testing best practices

---

### Business Analyst

#### Role Summary
Captures requirements, maps business processes, and supports documentation. Bridges the gap between stakeholder needs and delivery teams.

#### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements
- Model and document business processes
- Write acceptance criteria and user stories
- Document business cases and ROI analysis
- Facilitate stakeholder alignment on requirements

#### Interactions
- **PdM**: Works closely to translate business needs into product requirements
- **PM**: Supports project planning with detailed requirement documentation
- **Stakeholders**: Facilitates discussions and captures needs from business users

---

### Support/Customer Success

#### Role Summary
Advocates for end-users, enables feedback loops, and supports product rollout and issue triage. Ensures customers achieve value from the product.

#### Responsibilities
- Manage user onboarding and training
- Respond to user issues and feedback
- Communicate releases, incidents, and updates to users
- Escalate product issues and feature requests
- Track customer satisfaction and success metrics

#### Interactions
- **PdM**: Shares user feedback and feature requests to inform the roadmap
- **QA**: Collaborates on triaging and reproducing user-reported issues
- **PM**: Provides project status updates and coordinates customer communications
- **SRE/Release**: Coordinates on incident communications and rollout timing
